About

A platform where your team will be amazing us with your idea and calibre of creating fantastic projects in the span of 24 hours along with the opportunity to build memories you could cherish later on the caffeine and snacks we'd gear you up with.

Hacktech is a Hack-A-Thon organized by Institution's Innovation Council(IIC) of Rajkiya Engineering College Banda. It's a non-stop product development competition using digital technologies, where problem statements are posed to students for innovative solutions. Innovation and entrepreneurship are at the peak in India. In order to make development a comprehensive mass movement and innovate on all fronts.

IIC REC Banda is conducting Hackathon, for identifying new and disruptive technology innovations to solve the challenges faced in our country. The shortlisted top teams will get a chance to develop a solution in 24 hours at the college campus. So gear up to collaborate and celebrate. We promise you the happiest hours of problem-solving! Exciting prizes are up for grabs!



Theme

Smart Campus

"Time is the most valuable thing that a man can spend"

So here we are going to solve real-life problems faced in the campus using digital technologies such as automates the processes of the campus for better efficiency and lower costs, Dynamic report dashboard, etc .

Agro Tech

"If the farmer is happy, then so is the nation"

So here we are going to solve real-life problems faced by the farmers using digital technologies such as getting a good price for their crop, sourcing the weather and climate-based data to decide the crop cycle etc.

Rule and Regulation

1.Code Of Conduct

All code must be written during the event. You cannot use existing code that you have written and finish it during the event.

1. Offensive language and misconduct will not be tolerated in any manner at the hackathon venue.
2. The organizers reserve the right to make changes to the event program without any prior notice, you will be notified for the things if there any changes take place.
3. Contestants should only use programming languages and tools allowed for each category.
4. All participants are strictly asked to stay in the main block of the college campus.
5. We are NOT responsible for any personal belongings, if any left behind.
6. Keep all the rooms clean and put back everything at their original positions.
7. Do not take anything that does not belong to you.
8. Consumption of any kind of hard drinks, tobacco and narcotics are NOT allowed.


2.Instructions

All participants must bring their own laptops, no computers will be provided.

1. All the participants must bring their sleeping kit and an extra pair of clothes (in case needed) along with them.
2. At least two team members must be physically present at all times at their allocated table.
3. Participants are requested not to change their allocated table.
4. It is compulsory for all the participants to bring their college/organization ID card for reporting and registration at campus.
5. Participants can bring snacks accordingly, although food and refreshments will be provided periodically at the venue itself.


3.Participation Criteria

The participation is open only to students and IT professionals with 0-3 years of experience.

1. All UG and PG students of any branch/discipline can participate.
2. The participants can be from anywhere across India.
3. There can be a Minimum of 2 members and maximum 4 members in a team.
4. One team can work on any one problem statement only.
5. To work on more than one problem statement, register again with a different team name.
6. All submissions should address the expectations given in the respective problem statements.
